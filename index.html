FAQ

Common questions

How quickly can you start?
Within 48 hours of signing the agreement. I onboard myself into your tools and rhythm without disrupting your workflow.
Is there a minimum contract period?
No minimum. All plans run month to month and can be cancelled with 14 days written notice. No penalties, no awkward conversations.
What tools do you work with?
Smartlead, LinkedIn, HubSpot, Asana, Notion, Monday, Zoho, Mailchimp, Canva, Google Workspace, and more. If you use it, I can likely work in it.
Can I change plans as my needs grow?
Absolutely. Your support adjusts as your business evolves. We review what's working and update scope as needed.
How do I pay?
All invoices are issued monthly via Payoneer. Payment is due within 7 days of the invoice date. All amounts are in USD.
Do you work with clients outside Kenya?
Yes. I work with clients in the US, UK, Canada, Australia, and beyond. Time zones have never been an issue — your business runs smoothly regardless of where you are.

Hi, I'm Whitney.

The calm behind the chaos.

I studied hospitality management — an industry built on anticipating what people need before they ask, managing a hundred moving parts at once, and making sure everyone feels taken care of. I didn't know it at the time, but that training would become the foundation of everything I do now.

In 2022, someone close to me introduced me to virtual assistance, and something clicked. The skills I'd built — staying organised under pressure, keeping things running behind the scenes, paying attention to the details other people miss — were exactly what business owners needed.

I started, landed my first client, and haven't stopped growing since. Today I support coaches, speakers, founders, and real estate professionals across operations, content, lead generation, and full business support — from Nairobi, Kenya, serving clients globally.

The mission

Why I'm building this

I'm building Whitney Ops because I believe in financial freedom — for myself, for my family, and eventually for the team I'll build around this.

Every client I serve is a step toward something bigger. What starts as one person supporting your business will grow into a full team — but the standard of care, attention, and reliability will always stay the same.

Beyond work

A little more about me

When I'm not working, you'll find me trying a new recipe in the kitchen, reading, listening to music, exploring new places, or spending time with my church community. I'm also passionate about building community — I believe we grow better when we grow together.

I'm based in Nairobi, Kenya, and I work with clients all over the world.

Working style

What it's like working with me

Calm, steady, reliable

Things get done when they're supposed to. Follow-ups don't disappear. You're not constantly checking in wondering what happened.

Detail-aware, big-picture minded

I handle details carefully but never lose sight of what matters. Small things don't turn into big distractions.

Proactive, not reactive

I don't wait to be told. If something needs attention, I flag it. If something's about to fall through, I catch it first.

Flexible as things evolve

As your business grows or changes, support adjusts with it. No rigid systems or unnecessary complexity.
